About Mario

Who is Mario?

What do the Secret Exits do?

The secret Exits in 1-3, 2-6, 3-Ghost House, 4-Mid Castle, 5-Ghost House, and 6-6 all unlock a canon which shoot you to a higher world, for example the cannon in 1-3 leads you to world 5. The rest of the secret exits lead you further into the same world, for example the secret exit in 8-2 will lead you to 8-8.

Save File Stars

  1. Star #1 - Beat 8-Final Castle
  2. Star #2 - Get all Star Coins in World 9
  3. Star #3 - Beat every level
  4. Star #4 - Get all star coins in World 1 - 8
  5. Star #5 - Beat every level, using every secret goal, and use all warp cannons
  6. 5 "Sparkly" Stars - Beat every level without dying 8 or more times on each level, and get all 5 stars
