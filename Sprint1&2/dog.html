About Dogs

History of Dogs

Dogs first appeared around 14,220 - 16,000 years ago as a descendent of the wolf, were first domesticated from 6,400 - 14,000 years ago, and were used as sled dogs to migrate to North America around 9,000 years ago.

What Dogs Like and Dislike

Most dogs like being pet on the neck, shoulders, or chest. Dogs also like playing, walking, and sleeping. Dogs dislike cats a lot and may chase your cat around. Dogs also dislike hugging, getting in its personal space, punishment, and not letting it go on walks.

Diet of a Dog

Dogs like to eat treats, meat, and plants. Most diets are good as long as it is healthy, and has the nutrition needed.
